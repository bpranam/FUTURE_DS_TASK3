# 📊 Conversion Performance & Marketing Funnel Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Conversion Performance & Marketing Funnel Analysis Dashboard** is a Power BI project designed to analyze **customer behavior, traffic sources, device usage, and conversion performance across a marketing funnel**.

This dashboard helps businesses understand how visitors move through the **sales funnel stages (Visitor → Add to Cart → Checkout → Customer)** and identify opportunities to improve **conversion rates and revenue generation**.

The report provides a clear visual representation of:

* Traffic source effectiveness
* Funnel drop-off points
* Device usage patterns
* Revenue distribution across product categories
* Time-based performance trends

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze **visitor conversion behavior**
* Identify **best-performing marketing channels**
* Track **conversion rates across funnel stages**
* Understand **device-wise user engagement**
* Monitor **revenue distribution by product category**
* Detect **performance trends over time**

---

# 📈 Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ Revenue by Product Category

Displays revenue generated from different product categories such as:

* Sports
* Home & Living
* Electronics
* Other / Unknown

This helps identify **top-performing product segments**.

---

## 2️⃣ Funnel Stage Analysis

Visualizes the **marketing conversion funnel**:

Visitor → Add to Cart → Checkout → Customer

This helps identify **drop-off points** and improve marketing strategies.

---

## 3️⃣ Traffic Source Performance

Compares **Total Visitors vs Total Customers** across traffic channels:

* Paid Ads
* Organic
* Social Media
* Email
* Referral
* Unknown

This enables marketers to **measure channel effectiveness**.

---

## 4️⃣ Conversion Rate by Funnel Stage

Tracks conversion percentages for:

* Cart %
* Checkout %
* Purchase %

Useful for optimizing the **customer journey and checkout process**.

---

## 5️⃣ Device Type Analysis

Breakdown of traffic by device type:

* Mobile
* Desktop
* Tablet
* Unknown

Helps determine **which devices drive the most engagement and conversions**.

---

## 6️⃣ Time-Based Trend Analysis

Shows monthly performance for:

* Total Visitors
* Total Customers

This helps identify **seasonal trends and marketing performance changes**.

---
